How It Works

1
Transcribe
Every episode is transcribed via Deepgram Nova-2 with word-level timestamps and speaker diarization
2
Segment
Claude Sonnet reads the full transcript and identifies each distinct topic discussion — like a producer breaking an episode into chapters
3
Match
Your topics are searched against every segment using OpenAI vector embeddings, semantic similarity, and keyword signals
4
Score & Filter
Claude Haiku scores each match for relevance, sustained discussion, and depth. Passing mentions, ads, and tangents are filtered out.
5
Trim
Clip boundaries are refined using keyword anchoring and LLM analysis so each clip starts at the topic introduction and ends at its natural resolution
→ Your Playlist
The exact conversations you care about, ready to listen
